Rubber Armstrong Stewards Sale Tickets (important)

Rubber Armstrong is allocated a limited number of Steward Sale tickets directly by the Burning Man Org.

If you want to be considered for Rubber Armstrong Stewards Sale tickets, you must submit the Rubber Armstrong Statement of Intent and select the ticket option for Stewards Sale consideration.

If you don't tick the box, you're not in the running. Simple as that.

Key points, no surprises:

  • If you're interested in potentially receiving a Steward Sale ticket allocation, indicate this in your Statement of Intent.
  • Expressing interest does not guarantee a ticket. Final allocation decisions are made by camp leadership based on contribution needs and camp capacity.
  • If allocated, tickets are assigned directly to you by email.
  • Tickets are sold in pairs, with an optional Vehicle Pass.
  • Once allocated, tickets must be purchased immediately during the Steward Sale window.
  • Unpurchased allocations are forfeited. No extensions. No exceptions.

These tickets are intended for contributors who actively support the camp and its infrastructure. Allocation is not guaranteed and is based on contribution needs for that year.

Important: You do not need a Burning Man ticket to submit an SOI. However, if approved, you will need to secure a ticket through any Burning Man sale (Steward, DGS, Main Sale, OMG Sale, or resale) to complete the Join process. We cannot guarantee tickets, and approval does not guarantee camp placement if you cannot secure a ticket.
