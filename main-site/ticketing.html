Steward Sale Tickets

Rubber Armstrong is allocated a limited number of Steward Sale tickets by Burning Man.

How it works:

  • Applied for via Statement of Intent
  • Allocated tickets are assigned by email
  • Sold in pairs, with optional Vehicle Pass
  • Must be purchased immediately during the Steward Sale window
  • Unpurchased allocations are forfeited. No extensions.

Tickets are for contributors who support camp infrastructure. Allocation is not guaranteed.

For full pricing, sale dates, and official policies, always refer to the Burning Man ticketing site:

https://tickets.burningman.org/
