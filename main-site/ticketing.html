Burning Man ticketing changes every year. We keep this page up to date, but final details always come from the Burning Man Project.

As soon as the Burning Man Org publishes official ticket information, this page will be updated immediately.

Rubber Armstrong Steward Sale Tickets

Rubber Armstrong is allocated a limited number of Steward Sale tickets directly by the Burning Man Org.

Key points, no surprises:

  • Steward Sale tickets are applied for via the Statement of Intent.
  • If allocated, tickets are assigned directly to you by email.
  • Tickets are sold in pairs, with an optional Vehicle Pass.
  • Once allocated, tickets must be purchased immediately during the Steward Sale window.
  • Unpurchased allocations are forfeited. No extensions. No exceptions.

These tickets are intended for contributors who actively support the camp and its infrastructure. Allocation is not guaranteed and is based on contribution needs for that year.

For full pricing, sale dates, and official policies, always refer to the Burning Man ticketing site:

https://tickets.burningman.org/

We will mirror confirmed information here as soon as it is released.
