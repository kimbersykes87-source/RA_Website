Join Rubber Armstrong

Established camp. Selective membership. Two-stage process.

Read this before applying.

Process

Two stages. Statement of Intent first. Operational intake after Steward Sale for approved applicants.

Stage 1: Statement of Intent

5 minutes. Contact details, burn history, likelihood of attending, Steward Sale interest, what you bring.

This is not a ticket. This is not a guarantee. Submitting a Statement of Intent starts a review process, nothing more.

Stage 2: Review

Submissions are reviewed manually.

Everyone will be contacted after the Steward Sale. Either with an offer to join or a notice that this year is not the right fit.

Selection criteria:

  • Intent to contribute
  • Realistic understanding of Burning Man
  • Reliability and follow-through
  • Alignment with camp operations

Timing and Tickets

  • SOI opens before tickets are finalised
  • "I Want to Join" form opens after Steward Sale
  • In recent years, Steward Sale has been mid-March
  • Burning Man runs ticket sales, not Rubber Armstrong
  • The camp does not sell tickets and cannot guarantee tickets

Everyone will be contacted after the Steward Sale.

Boundaries

  • Submitting an SOI does not mean you are in
  • Approval means you may be invited to the next step once tickets are secured
  • This is not plug-and-play. This is not low-responsibility. This is not for everyone

Ready to Start?
