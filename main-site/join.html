Join Rubber Armstrong

Rubber Armstrong is a long-standing Burning Man camp built around contribution, reliability, and shared responsibility. We don't run on first-come-first-served, and we don't over-promise. This page explains how joining actually works.

Read it properly before applying.

How Joining Works

Joining Rubber Armstrong is a two-stage process, aligned with the Burning Man ticket cycle.

Stage 1: Statement of Intent (SOI)

The Statement of Intent is the first step.

  • Takes around 5 minutes
  • Collects basic contact details
  • Covers high-level logistics and availability
  • Helps us understand who you are, what you bring, and why you want to be part of the camp

This is not a full application and does not require you to have a ticket.

The SOI helps us build an early picture of interest and values alignment before ticketing settles.

Stage 2: Review Period

After submitting your SOI:

  • Submissions are reviewed manually
  • The review period typically takes 4‚Äì8 weeks
  • We may not contact everyone
  • Submitting an SOI does not guarantee a place

We prioritise people who demonstrate:

  • Clear intent to contribute
  • Realistic understanding of Burning Man
  • Reliability, effort, and follow-through
  • Alignment with how the camp operates

Timing and Tickets (Read This Carefully)

  • The SOI opens before tickets are finalised
  • The formal "I Want to Join" intake opens only after the Steward Sale has completed
  • In 2025, this was mid-March
  • Ticket sales are run entirely by Burning Man and are separate from Rubber Armstrong
  • We do not sell tickets publicly and we cannot guarantee tickets

If you are approved to move forward, you will be contacted with next steps after major ticket sales have concluded.

Important Notes

  • Submitting an SOI does not mean you are in the camp
  • Approval means you may be invited to complete the next step once tickets are secured
  • If you are looking for guaranteed tickets, plug-and-play camping, or low responsibility, this camp is not for you
