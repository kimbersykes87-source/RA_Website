Rubber Armstrong

Named in cosmic tribute to humanity's giant leap, Rubber Armstrong honours a crew whose arms need barely twisting to launch spontaneously into journeys of wonder. Like astronauts bound not for the stars, but for adventures wild and whimsical, we eagerly orbit moments that stretch our imaginations.

Welcome aboard - you've joined a voyage where every "yes" ignites the engines of unforgettable escapades.

What We Bring to the Playa

Shade & Infrastructure

Spacious, insulated tents with 120V power. Aluminium shade cloth that blocks UV while allowing hot air to escape. Designed for comfort in extreme conditions.

Solar Power

Sustainable, off-grid solar systems powering the entire camp. From tent AC to the art car, we run on sunshine and good intentions.

Mezcal Bar & Radiance Hour

Our gift to the playa: a communal Mezcal bar. Daily Radiance Hour where campmates share skills, stories, and hacks in a super-casual setting.

SS Mezcal Art Car

Solar-powered mobile platform for exploration and connection. Flagship of rubber-fueled revelry and questionable navigational choices.

Water & Showers

5,000L of filtered drinking water. Daily disciplined showers with burner-friendly soap. Water is the driving force of all nature.

Leave No Trace

Comprehensive recycling system. Everything we consume is taken out and recycled. MOOP sweeps, sustainable practices, environmental focus.

We are a weird and wonderful family. We support each other, we respect each other, and we work together to co-exist harmoniously.

Learn About Our Values

A Working Camp, Not a Hotel

Rubber Armstrong is a participatory community. Everyone signs up for 1-2 shifts (bar, kitchen, recycling, art car driving etc.). Everyone contributes. Everyone is measured as a collective.

"However small your contribution may seem, it is felt. Teamwork makes the dream work."
