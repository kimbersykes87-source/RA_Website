Two-stage process. Statement of Intent first. Operational intake after Steward Sale for approved applicants.

Contact details, burn history, likelihood of attending, Steward Sale interest, what you bring.

What We Bring to the Playa

▭ Shade & Infrastructure

Insulated tents with 120V power. Aluminium shade cloth blocks UV, allows hot air to escape.

◆ Solar Power

Off-grid solar powers the entire camp. Tent AC, art car, infrastructure.

○ Mezcal Bar & Radiance Hour

Communal Mezcal bar. Daily Radiance Hour for skills, stories, practical knowledge.

◇ SS Mezcal Art Car

Solar-powered art car. Mobile platform for playa exploration.

◈ Water & Showers

5,000L filtered drinking water. Daily showers with burner-friendly soap.

△ Leave No Trace

Everything consumed is taken out and recycled. Daily MOOP sweeps.

People take care of each other. People take care of the camp. This is how it works.

How the Camp Operates

Working Camp

Everyone signs up for 1-2 shifts. Bar, kitchen, recycling, art car. Everyone contributes.

This is not a hotel. This is not a service.
