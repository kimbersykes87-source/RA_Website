## Step-by-Step Implementation Guide: YAMM

### Phase 1: Setup (15 minutes)

1. **Install YAMM**
   - Go to [Google Workspace Marketplace](https://workspace.google.com/marketplace)
   - Search for "Yet Another Mail Merge"
   - Click "Install" and grant necessary permissions
   - Choose "YAMM Classic" or "YAMM Campaigns" (either works)

2. **Sign Up for Starter Plan**
   - Open Gmail and look for YAMM in your add-ons
   - Click to start a campaign
   - Sign up for Starter plan ($25/month for 400 emails/day)
   - Note: You can cancel after your campaign

3. **Prepare Your Recipient List**
   - Create a new Google Sheet
   - **Required columns:**
     - `Email` - Recipient email addresses
     - `First Name` - For personalization (optional)
     - `Last Name` - For personalization (optional)
     - `Status` - YAMM will fill this in automatically
   - **Example:**
     ```
     | Email                    | First Name | Last Name |
     |--------------------------|------------|-----------|
     | person1@example.com      | John       | Doe       |
     | person2@example.com      | Jane       | Smith     |
     ```

### Phase 2: Create Your Campaign (30 minutes)

4. **Compose Your Email**
   - Open your Google Sheet with recipients
   - Click "Extensions" ‚Üí "Yet Another Mail Merge" ‚Üí "Start mail merge"
   - Or click the YAMM icon in Gmail
   - Compose your Burning Man invitation email

5. **Add Personalization**
   - Use merge tags: `{{First Name}}` or `{{FirstName}}`
   - Example: "Dear {{First Name}}," instead of "Dear Friend,"
   - Preview to make sure merge tags work correctly

6. **Insert Your Email Content**
   - Copy your current invitation email
   - Make sure all links are included
   - Add any attachments (PDFs, Statement of Intent form, etc.)

7. **Enable Tracking**
   - In YAMM settings, ensure "Email tracking" is enabled
   - This adds invisible tracking pixels automatically
   - Links are automatically tracked

### Phase 3: Test Before Sending (10 minutes)

8. **Send Test Emails**
   - Add 2-3 test email addresses to your sheet (your own emails)
   - Run a test mail merge to these addresses only
   - Check that:
     - Emails arrive correctly
     - Personalization works
     - Links work
     - Formatting looks good
     - Tracking shows up in YAMM dashboard

### Phase 4: Send Your Campaign (5 minutes)

9. **Review Your Recipients**
   - Double-check your 299 email addresses
   - Remove any duplicates
   - Verify all email addresses are valid

10. **Send the Campaign**
    - Click "Send emails" in YAMM
    - Choose send immediately or schedule
    - **Recommended**: Send on Tuesday-Thursday, 10am-2pm for best open rates
    - Confirm and send

11. **Monitor Initial Sends**
    - Watch for first 10-20 emails
    - Check for bounces or errors
    - If any issues, pause and fix

### Phase 5: Track and Follow Up (Ongoing)

12. **View Your Tracking Dashboard**
    - Go to your Google Sheet
    - YAMM updates the "Status" column automatically:
      - `EMAIL_SENT` - Sent successfully
      - `EMAIL_OPENED` - Recipient opened
      - `LINK_CLICKED` - Recipient clicked a link
      - `EMAIL_BOUNCED` - Email failed
    - Or view YAMM's web dashboard for visual reports

13. **Identify Non-Openers**
    - After 3-5 days, filter your sheet:
      - Status = `EMAIL_SENT` (not opened)
    - Export this list for follow-up
    - Send a gentle reminder email to non-openers

14. **Export Your Data**
    - Download your Google Sheet as CSV
    - You now have a complete record of:
      - Who received the email
      - Who opened it
      - Who clicked links
      - When they engaged

## Best Practices for Avoiding Spam Filters

### Email Content Tips

1. **Avoid Spam Trigger Words**
   - ‚ùå Avoid: "Free", "Click here", "Act now", "Limited time"
   - ‚úÖ Use: Natural, personal language

2. **Keep a Good Text-to-Image Ratio**
   - Include substantial text content
   - Don't send image-only emails

3. **Include a Physical Address**
   - Add your camp's address or contact info
   - Shows legitimacy

4. **Provide Unsubscribe Option**
   - Since this is an invitation to known contacts, you can include:
   - "If you'd like to be removed from future Rubber Armstrong updates, please reply with 'unsubscribe'"

### Sending Strategy

5. **Warm Up Your Account**
   - If your Gmail account is new, send smaller batches first
   - Build sending reputation over a few days

6. **Send at Optimal Times**
   - **Best days**: Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday
   - **Best times**: 10am-2pm recipient's time zone
   - Avoid Monday mornings and Friday afternoons

7. **Pace Your Sends**
   - Even though you CAN send 299 at once, consider:
   - Sending 100-150 per day over 2-3 days
   - Reduces spam risk
   - Makes responses more manageable

8. **Use a Professional "From" Name**
   - Use "Rubber Armstrong Camp" instead of personal name
   - Or "Your Name - Rubber Armstrong"

9. **Authenticate Your Domain**
   - If you have a custom domain, set up SPF/DKIM
   - Increases deliverability

## Tracking Pixels: How It Works

All these tools use invisible tracking pixels:

1. **What is a Tracking Pixel?**
   - A 1x1 transparent image embedded in your email
   - Unique URL for each recipient
   - When email is opened, pixel loads, server logs the open

2. **When Tracking Works:**
   - ‚úÖ Recipient has images enabled (most modern email clients)
   - ‚úÖ Email is opened in Gmail, Outlook, Apple Mail
   - ‚úÖ Recipient views email in preview pane or opens it

3. **When Tracking May Fail:**
   - ‚ùå Recipient has images disabled
   - ‚ùå Email is read in plain text mode
   - ‚ùå Strict privacy email clients (some newer iOS features)
   - ‚ùå Corporate firewalls that block tracking

4. **Typical Accuracy**
   - Open tracking: ~85-90% accurate
   - Click tracking: ~95% accurate (harder to block)

## Troubleshooting Common Issues

### Issue: Emails Going to Spam

**Solutions:**
- Send smaller batches (50-100 at a time)
- Warm up your account by sending to engaged users first
- Avoid spam trigger words
- Make sure SPF/DKIM are configured
- Ask recipients to add you to contacts

### Issue: Low Open Rates

**Solutions:**
- Improve subject line (make it personal and urgent)
- Send at different time of day
- Send follow-up with different subject line
- Check if emails are landing in Promotions tab (Gmail)

### Issue: Tracking Not Working

**Solutions:**
- Verify extension is enabled
- Check recipient's email client allows images
- Send test email to yourself
- Try different browser (Chrome works best)

### Issue: Can't Send to 299 at Once

**Solutions:**
- Check you haven't hit Gmail's daily limit
- Split into batches over 2 days
- Upgrade to Google Workspace for higher limits
